Bundle Block Adjustment With Constrained Relative Orientation

with E. Maset, L. Magri, I. Toschi

Overview

This paper deals with bundle adjustment with constrained cameras, i.e. where the orientation of certain cameras is expressed relatively to others, and these relative orientations are part of the unknowns. We provide experimental evidence that indeed these constraints improve the accuracy of the results, while reducing the computational load as well.
